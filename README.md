# Winchu · Nexus ⚡

**Shape your presence. Manifest your energy.**

A full-featured progressive web application (PWA) for social networking, wellness tracking, and personal growth.

---

## 🌟 Features

### Social & Community
- **Social Feed** - Share posts with images and videos
- **Video Feed** - TikTok-style vertical video browsing
- **Stories/Status** - Share 24-hour disappearing statuses
- **Likes & Comments** - Engage with community content
- **Bookmarks** - Save posts and videos for later
- **User Profiles** - View other users' posts and profiles
- **Friend System** - Send and accept friend requests
- **Notifications** - Real-time alerts for interactions

### Communication
- **Private Chats** - One-on-one direct messaging
- **Group Chats** - Create and manage groups with friends
- **File Attachments** - Share images, videos, and files
- **Location Sharing** - Send your GPS location in chats
- **Global Chat** - Community chat for all users

### Wellness & Productivity
- **Aura System** - Choose 3 energy types that define your tasks
- **Dashboard** - Track streaks, tasks, and daily progress
- **Task Management** - Complete daily tasks based on your auras
- **Calendar** - Visual streak tracking calendar
- **📖 Diary** - Beautiful book-style diary with PIN protection
- **📋 Routines** - Create, manage, and track daily routines
- **Tags & Search** - Organize routines with tags

### Personalization
- **🎨 Wallpapers** - 100+ curated wallpapers (portrait & landscape)
- **Custom Avatars** - Upload your profile picture
- **Bio & Name** - Customize your profile display
- **Aura Selection** - Choose from 8 different energy types
